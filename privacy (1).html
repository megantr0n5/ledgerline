Ledgerline · Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated July 15, 2026

The short version

Ledgerline keeps your financial records on your phone. Your balances, income, bills and dates are stored on the device and are never sent to us, because there is no server to send them to. We have no account system, so we do not know who you are.

  • No account, no email, no password
  • No bank connection and no bank credentials — you type in the numbers yourself
  • No advertising, no ad identifiers, no tracking across apps
  • No analytics SDK

This policy explains what Ledgerline (“the app”, “we”, “us”) does with information when you use it. Ledgerline is published by Nicholas Vo.

1Information you enter

Everything you type into Ledgerline — your starting balance, income and expense records, amounts, dates, repeat schedules, per-date adjustments, and your low-point alert setting — is stored locally on your device using the operating system’s standard app storage.

This information is not transmitted to us, is not backed up to our servers, and is not shared with any third party. We cannot see it. If your device is backed up to iCloud or Google, that backup is governed by Apple’s or Google’s privacy policy, not this one.

2Information collected when you buy

Purchases are processed by Apple or Google. We never see or receive your card number, billing address, or payment credentials.

We use RevenueCat to manage purchases and unlock the paid version. When you buy or restore a purchase, RevenueCat processes the following on our behalf:

DataWhy
A randomly generated app user IDTo attach your purchase to your install. It is not linked to your name or email.
Purchase receipt and subscription statusTo verify the purchase is genuine and to unlock the app.
Device model, operating system version, countrySent automatically by the store when validating a receipt.
IP addressReceived as part of the network request; used to determine country and prevent fraud.

RevenueCat acts as a data processor for us. None of your financial records are sent to RevenueCat — only the purchase information above.

3Your free trial

Ledgerline gives you a 14-day trial without asking for payment details. To do that, the app stores the date your trial started in your device’s secure storage (the iOS Keychain, or its Android equivalent).

Please note: on iOS, this single date is kept in the Keychain, which by design survives deleting the app. This is so that reinstalling does not silently restart the trial. It is one timestamp. It contains no personal information and is not sent anywhere. You can remove it by erasing your device, or by contacting us.

4What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or bank login details. Ledgerline contains no advertising, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not sell or share personal information, and we do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

5Fonts and network use

Ledgerline’s typefaces are bundled inside the app. The app does not load fonts, scripts, or other assets from third-party servers at runtime. Apart from verifying purchases, Ledgerline works fully offline.

6Children

Ledgerline is intended for adults managing their own money. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7How long data is kept

Your records stay on your device until you delete them or delete the app. We hold no copy. Purchase records are retained by RevenueCat and by Apple or Google for as long as needed to support your purchase, honour restores, and meet tax and accounting obligations.

8Your rights

Depending on where you live — for example under the GDPR in the UK and EU, or the CCPA in California — you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete personal information we hold about you, and to object to processing.

In practice, the information you would most want to control is already entirely in your hands: it lives on your device, and deleting the app deletes it. For purchase records, or to request removal of the trial timestamp described in section 3, contact us at theledgerlineapp@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

The legal basis for processing purchase data is the performance of our contract with you (providing the app you bought) and our legitimate interest in preventing fraud.

9Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where the change is significant, note it in the app. Continuing to use Ledgerline after a change means you accept the updated policy.
